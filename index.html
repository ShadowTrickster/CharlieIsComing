Charlie's Origin

Charlie was an adorable frog that lived in a peaceful pound with his friends.
Charlie had the dream of some day becoming famous, a star like those in the movies he watched through the window of the old man's cabin near the pound.
One day one of Charlie's friend had an excelent idea, what about becoming a so called webpage?
And this is how Charlie trascended reason and logic and became this website.
It is thanks to you, reader, that Charlie is able to be famous like he wished back in the pound.
One wonders whats the price that Charlie had to pay for such a magnificent opportunity.
But some times is better to not know the answer.
Those that dig down the answer report whispers of Charlie in the night, accompanied by countless frogs in the backyard.
It is my duty as a reader and one of Charlie's victims to warn you about the dangers of this webpage.
Stay away from here before is too late.
